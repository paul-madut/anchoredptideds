Things Worth Knowing
Reconstitute Correctly
Use bacteriostatic water every time. Keep peptides at 2–8°C after mixing. Don't freeze them once reconstituted.
Track Your Protein
Retatrutide suppresses appetite hard. Deliberately track protein to hit 1g per lb of bodyweight. Don't assume you're eating enough just because you feel full.
Get Bloodwork Done
Baseline before starting and check again at the midpoint. Glucose, lipids, liver markers. No exceptions, regardless of how you're feeling.
Inject Site Rotation
Running this many subq compounds means rotating injection sites is non-negotiable. Cycle between abdomen, thigh, and the back of the arm to keep tissue healthy.
GI on Retatrutide
Can cause mild nausea, especially early on or when adjusting dose. Smaller meals and avoiding high-fat foods around injection day helps. It passes quickly.
Hydration & Electrolytes
Running this many compounds in a caloric deficit makes hydration critical. Daily electrolytes make a clear difference in training performance and recovery.
